Callum is a Transport Planner with Arup based in Melbourne, Australia.

Having worked both remotely and on site for airport strategy, planning and simulation projects across Australasia, China, North America and the Middle East, Callum has developed a broad skillset enabling project involvement from high level strategic forecasting though to design and operation.

With this experience Callum can provide valuable guidance and technical input to ensure that the most innovative, efficient and effective outcomes are achieved for the clients, stakeholders and end users.

Through his understanding of the challenges faced by the urban environment, within the context of an airport city both now and into the future, Callum plans and designs infrastructure solutions that help to create not only better journeys but also places for people.
